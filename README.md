# Sales Performance & Revenue Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, product category trends, and regional business performance. The dashboard helps business users quickly monitor key performance indicators, identify revenue trends, and understand which categories and regions are driving business results.

## Business Problem
Startup leadership and sales teams need a simple way to track business performance across sales, profit, quantity, profit margin, regions, and product categories. The goal of this dashboard is to convert raw sales data into clear insights that support faster and more informed business decisions.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- DAX
- Data Visualization
- Business Intelligence Reporting

## Key Performance Indicators
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Quantity
- Profit Margin
- Sales Trend by Date
- Sales by Region
- Sales by Product Category
- Category and Region Performance

## Dashboard Features
- KPI cards for total sales, total profit, total quantity, and profit margin
- Line chart to analyze sales trends over time
- Bar chart to compare sales performance by region
- Pie chart to review product category contribution
- Region slicer for interactive filtering
- Performance tables for category and regional analysis

## Data Preparation
- Reviewed and cleaned sales data for dashboard reporting
- Validated sales, profit, quantity, and category fields
- Structured data for KPI calculations and visual analysis
- Created measures to summarize business performance
- Designed visuals for executive-level reporting

## Business Insights
- Sales trends help identify business growth patterns over time.
- Regional sales analysis helps identify stronger and weaker performing markets.
- Category-level analysis highlights which product groups contribute most to revenue.
- Profit margin tracking helps determine whether revenue growth is converting into profitable business performance.
- Interactive filtering allows users to explore performance by region and category.

## Business Recommendations
- Focus sales and marketing efforts on high-performing regions and product categories.
- Investigate underperforming regions to identify gaps in customer engagement or sales execution.
- Monitor profit margin regularly to ensure sales growth remains profitable.
- Use monthly sales trends to support forecasting, planning, and leadership reporting.
